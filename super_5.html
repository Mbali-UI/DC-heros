DARKSEID
Formerly known as Uxas, Darkseid is a New God and the tyrannical ruler of the planet Apokolips.
His ultimate goal is to find and gain control over the Anti-Life Equation
to enslave the multiverse by eliminating all hope and free will in sentient beings.
He is the archenemy of Superman and the Justice League, and is one of the most powerful beings in the DC Universe.
