DC Comics DC Comics (originally DC Comics, Inc., and also known simply as DC) is an American comic book publisher owned by DC Entertainment, a subsidiary of Warner Bros. Discovery. DC is an initialism for "Detective Comics", an American comic book series first published in 1937


DC Comics is one of the largest and oldest American comic book companies, the first comic under the DC banner being published in 1937.
The majority of its publications are set in the fictional DC Universe and feature numerous culturally iconic heroic characters, such as Superman, Batman, Wonder Woman, Green Lantern, the Flash, Aquaman, Martian Manhunter, and Cyborg; as well as famous fictional teams, including the Justice League, the Justice Society of America, the Teen Titans, and the Suicide Squad.
The universe contains an assortment of well-known supervillains, such as Lex Luthor, the Joker, the Cheetah, Deathstroke, the Reverse-Flash, Black Manta, Brainiac, and Darkseid.
